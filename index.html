How to Play

Each row is a 3-word chain.

Word 2 must be an anagram of Word 1 plus the first given letter.

Word 3 must be an anagram of Word 2 plus the second given letter.

Example: RATE + S → STARE + W → WATERS

Green means confirmed.
Yellow means not fully judgeable yet.
Red means something is wrong.

You can keep editing to use more letters, even after you're done.
